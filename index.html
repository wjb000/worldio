How to Play WorldIO

Your digital civilization awaits

Move & Interact
Click anywhere on the world to move your Founder. Use WASD keys too.
Tools at the bottom
Switch between Gather, Build, Spawn, and Trade. Each click performs the action at that location.
Propagate & Share
Export your thriving world and share the JSON with friends. Or fork the entire repo and launch your own variant instantly.
The world evolves even when you're away. Come back and see how much it has grown.
